**Resale and refurbishment**

Their predicted stance is conditional: the extension is good only if planned obsolescence and repairability are addressed. That adds a **trust dimension** to the ecosystem: developer and consumer benefit from the extended lifecycle only if Apple’s behaviour on software and repairability is seen as credible.
